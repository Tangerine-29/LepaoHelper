# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

We are committed to a respectful and harassment-free community.

Examples of positive behavior:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on technical discussions

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or discrimination
- Personal attacks and trolling
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, or contributions that violate this code.
Repeated or severe violations may result in a ban from participation.

## Reporting

Please report behavior issues privately to the maintainers.
